# ESP32-Acoustic-Anomaly-Detection-System
SmartEar is an AI powered anomaly detection system that listens to industrial machine sounds in real-time to predict equipment failures before they occur

1.Predictive Maintenance : Reduces downtime ,maintenance costs ,Increases equipment lifespan and operational efficiency.
2.Safety & Risk Mitigation :  Prevents catastrophic failures before they happen (machinery failure, structural collapses) and protects worker safety in high-risk environments.
3.Sustainability : Optimizes resource usage by reducing unnecessary maintenance runs and prevents waste from premature equipment replacement.
4.Real-Time Decision Making : Edge AI makes it possible for millisecond response times (no internet latency) and Operators get alerts instantly to take corrective action
